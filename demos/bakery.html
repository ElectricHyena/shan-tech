Our Story

The Little Bakehouse started as a dream in my grandmother's kitchen. Growing up, I watched her transform simple ingredients into magical creations that brought our family together.

In 2018, I finally took the leap and opened our little bakery in the heart of Milton Keynes. Today, we continue those traditions, baking everything by hand, from scratch, every single day.

We believe that good bread takes time, quality ingredients, and a whole lot of love. That's exactly what goes into everything we make.
